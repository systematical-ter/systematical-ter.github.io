Oh I forgot to mention earlier:

I fixed pagination on my blog so that's good. The posts in hidden section still shows up on pages that aren't the first page, but that should be an easy enough fix. Basically, for some reason the index.html source included two pagination starters, so literally all I had to do was delete one of them. I also changed the pagination template itself so look a little nicer -- I changed it from being a vertical <ul> object with entries for first, prev, cur, next, and last to cramming all five of those things into a single row. I think it looks nice.

I do also need to still see if it's paginating at the right count of posts, but I haven't bothered counting the posts.

bbcf

Only a small comment, otherwise I'd make it a new post as usual. My playstyle really suits playing Jin and I don't know how to feel about that. Looking back at some of my games, sure I'm making the usual mistakes I do on Terumi, but they work out a lot better because of Jin's kit -- and in fact, sometimes are really good strategies for Jin. So I don't know how I feel about that. I'm definitely going to keep Jin as a sub-character, but if a single person tells me that my Jin is better than my Terumi I'm actually going to walk into the ocean to never be seen again. I'll find all the tea we threw into the harbor forever ago.
